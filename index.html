I do this as a Strategy, Ops and AI Transformation operator. I bring 15+ years experience across start-ups, big tech, consulting, restructuring, and in-house transformations. Transformation & AI adoption
Enabling new capability. Practical AI adoption that fits the actual work. Finding and fixing issues in a structured way. Restructuring for bigger, faster and/or more profitable business.
My operating principles
  • Visibility as a system. Public channels, one-page docs, a central knowledge base. A trail of progress anyone can tap into.
  • Build over theory. Now beats later, and great ideas on paper aren't always great in reality. Prototype, ship, and get real-world feedback fast. Momentum is infectious.
  • Clear north stars. Decisions get made on the best and fastest path to the goal. And if the goal needs to change, it changes - that's just real-time learning.
  • Eliminate ruthlessly. Clarity comes from cutting, not adding. If it's not hell yes, it's probably a no.
  • Basics before sexy. Shiny ideas are great, but it's the boring details that often matter in the long-term. Be mindful of the foundations in place.
  • Diagnose upstream. Downstream symptoms - churn, complaints, support tickets - almost always start upstream.
  • AI-first lens. It's just the future. Start here - how do processes, teams, and products look through this lens? Just don't lose the human bit.
  • Decisive by default. Clear recommendations, room to disagree. "Moving this way unless there's a strong view otherwise."
  • Embrace tension. It comes with the territory when you're trying to do something new. Find what you agree on, table the differences, and agree how to resolve them - a test, a trial, a customer signal.
